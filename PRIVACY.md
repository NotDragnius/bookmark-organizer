# Privacy Policy for Sifted

**Effective Date:** March 5, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Sifted. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. This extension is designed primarily for personal use and a small group of friends.

## 2. Data We Collect
Sifted collects the following information strictly to provide its core functionality (bookmark tracking and organization):
*   **Bookmarks:** The extension reads your bookmarks, specifically within the "To Sort" folder, to capture URLs and titles you wish to organize.
*   **Authentication Data:** We store a minimal session identifier locally (user ID and email) to link your captured bookmarks to your specific account on our backend.

## 3. How We Use Your Data
The data we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:
*   To automatically send bookmarks placed in the "To Sort" folder to your personal account in our secure database (Supabase).
*   To ensure that the bookmarks being saved are correctly attributed to your user profile.

*We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.*

## 4. Data Storage and Security
Your captured bookmarks are sent directly to our Supabase backend database via a secure HTTPS connection. The extension itself does not permanently store your browsing history or bookmarks locally beyond temporary session data.

## 5. Third-Party Services
We use Supabase as our backend database provider. Please refer to [Supabase's Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy) for information on how they handle data stored on their servers.

## 6. User Consent
By installing and using the Sifted extension, you consent to the collection and use of your data as described in this Privacy Policy. The extension explicitly requests permissions for `bookmarks` and `storage` upon installation.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Since this is a personal project, any significant changes will be communicated directly to active users.

## 8. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please create an issue on our GitHub repository.
